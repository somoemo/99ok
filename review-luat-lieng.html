1. Tong quan ve Luat Lieng

Lieng la mot tro choi bai dan gian pho bien o Viet Nam. Day la mot tro choi don gian, de choi nhung lai rat hap dan va doi hoi nguoi choi phai co ky nang va may man. Tro choi nay duoc choi voi mot bo bai Tay thong thuong gom 52 la va 4 nguoi choi.

Theo Luat Lieng, moi nguoi choi se duoc nhan 5 la bai va can sap xep thanh ba nhom bai: ba la dau tien, ba la giua va hai la cuoi cung. Muc tieu cua tro choi la sap xep ba nhom bai sao cho tong gia tri cua ba nhom la so lon nhat. 2. Cac dieu can luu y khi choi Lieng

De co the choi Lieng thanh cong, ban can luu y nhung dieu sau day:

  • Thien lanh thien manh: Doi khi, ban se may man nhan duoc bai ba la dau tien co tong gia tri la 20 hoac 30. Dieu nay cung dong nghia voi viec ban da co mot loi the lon trong tro choi, vi ban chi can chon them hai la bai co tong gia tri la 10 hoac 20 la co the thang duoc.
  • Chon bai can than: Trong tro choi Lieng, ban can phai chon bai mot cach can than, suy nghi ky truoc khi quyet dinh. Neu khong, ban co the bi "chat chem" boi doi thu neu ho co bai tot hon.

Ngoai viec la mot tro choi giai tri thu vi, viec choi Lieng con mang lai nhieu loi ich cho nguoi choi:

  • Thien chi voi nhau: Choi Lieng la mot hoat dong giup moi nguoi gan ket va co nhung giay phut thu gian, vui ve cung nhau. Tro choi nay cung giup tao nen moi quan he tot dep giua cac thanh vien trong gia dinh hoac ban be.
